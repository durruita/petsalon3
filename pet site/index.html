Raising the bar in pet care, The K9 Cleaner offers full grooming, boarding and daycare. Relaxing music, aromatherapy, beautifully designed playrooms and individual over night Villas for your pet to enjoy along with other amenities will give your pet the true spa “experience”. We also offer maintenance packages to keep your pet feeling and smelling fresh between grooming visits and we can even wash your pet’s favorite bed.
We also offer a chauffeur service to transport your pet in style with the utmost safety to and from their home. Our trained staff will monitor your pets stay to ensure the most fun and stress free vacation. Web cams allow pet parents to check in and see their furry family member enjoying playtime with friends. K9 Cleaners has 24 hour staff to ensure the best possible care. The K9 Cleaners strives to create a calming atmosphere for your pets peace of mind.
